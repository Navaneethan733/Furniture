Quick Help

Common Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days within India. For custom or large items, it may take 10-14 days. You'll receive a tracking link as soon as your order leaves our workshop.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship primarily within India. However, for large bulk or trade orders, we can arrange international freight. Please contact our trade team for more details.

What is your return policy?

We want you to love your STACKLY pieces. We offer a 14-day hassle-free return policy for standard items in original condition. Custom-made furniture is generally final sale but covered by our 10-year warranty.

Are your materials sustainably sourced?

Yes. 100% of our solid timber is FSC-certified. We also use water-based, non-toxic finishes and recycled packaging materials wherever possible to minimize our footprint.
