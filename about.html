Crafting Quiet Spaces

Since 2012, STACKLY has been on a mission to bring intentional design and sustainable craftsmanship into every modern home.

Since 2012

Rooted in Quality, Driven by Design

What started as a small artisan workshop in Tamil Nadu has grown into a global destination for minimalist furniture. We believe that your home should be a reflection of your values—sustainable, functional, and beautifully simple.

Our journey began with a single solid oak chair. Today, we offer a full range of home and office solutions, each crafted with the same obsession for detail that defined our very first piece.

Common Questions

About Our Brand

Who designs STACKLY furniture?

Every piece is designed in-house by our creative team led by Sarah Miller. We collaborate with ergonomic experts and local artisans to ensure every product meets our high standards of form and function.

Where is your furniture manufactured?

Our main production workshop is located in Salem, Tamil Nadu. By keeping our manufacturing local, we can maintain strict quality control and support our community of talented craftspeople.

What makes your furniture "sustainable"?

We use FSC-certified solid woods, low-VOC finishes, and minimal plastic. Our "Built for Life" philosophy also means our furniture is designed to last decades, not years, reducing the impact of "fast furniture" on the environment.

Can I visit your workshop?

Yes! We host monthly "Open Workshop" days where customers can see our process first-hand. Please sign up for our newsletter or contact our team to reserve a spot.
